Our story

Picerija sredi zelenih gričev Selške doline

A pizzeria in the green hills of the Selca Valley

Marco Polo je picerija in gostilna v Trebiji pri Gorenji vasi, kjer se cestna ovinka sreča s pogledom na griče in domačije. Na pokriti terasi postrežemo pice iz krušne peči, sočne zrezke, kalamare in sveže solatne krožnike.

Marco Polo is a pizzeria and tavern in Trebija near Gorenja vas, where the road bends into a view of green hills and farmhouses. On our covered terrace we serve wood-style pizzas, juicy steaks, calamari and fresh salad plates.

Vsak delovnik med 9. in 14. uro pripravimo tudi malico po dnevnem izboru — odlično postanek za popotnike in domačine na poti skozi dolino.

Every weekday from 9am to 2pm we also prepare a daily lunch menu — a great stop for travellers and locals passing through the valley.

Visit & contact

Picerija Marco Polo stoji ob glavni cesti skozi Trebijo, le streljaj od Gorenje vasi — lahko dostopna z avtom, s parkiriščem tik pred vrati.

Marco Polo Pizzeria sits right on the main road through Trebija, just outside Gorenja vas — easy to reach by car, with parking right outside.
